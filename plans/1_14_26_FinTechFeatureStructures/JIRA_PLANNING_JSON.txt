I. Systemic Philosophy & Foundation (Phases 0-15)

Strategic Context & Analytical Prose

The genesis of the AI_Investor platform represents a fundamental transition from a linear "Job_Recruiter" infrastructure to a Complex Adaptive System. This architectural evolution is governed by the "Yellowstone Wolf Principle," where the introduction of autonomous agents—specifically the Searcher and Protector personas—restores equilibrium to the financial ecosystem. The system’s nervous system is comprised of a high-throughput Redpanda (Kafka) cluster for market telemetry, a TimescaleDB (Postgres) hypertable layer for time-series price persistence, and a Neo4j graph database to map the hidden "biological" correlations between global assets.

"So What?" Layer

The implementation of a dedicated Demo Account infrastructure in Phase 6 is not a merely academic exercise; it is a critical prophylactic against "internal sepsis." By simulating losses using "fake money," the system validates its homeostasis logic without risking real capital. In a predatory market environment, the ability to fail safely allows the AI to calibrate its sensory apparatus—pip/pipette precision and decimal scaling—ensuring that when real-world liquidity is engaged, the system acts with institutional-grade accuracy.

With the foundational nervous system established and the telemetry of Major Pairs synchronized, the roadmap transitions from environmental setup to the mathematical rigor of the Warden Protocol.

II. Risk Guardrails & The "Warden" Protocol (Phases 16-35)

Strategic Context & Analytical Prose

The Warden Protocol is the primary homeostatic regulator of the AI_Investor ecosystem. In this phase, we move beyond infrastructure to the "Prime Directive": Capital Preservation. The system is governed by the 1% Rule, a non-negotiable position-sizing constraint that ensures no single market oscillation can cause systemic sepsis. By automating the liquidation hierarchy through the ProtectorAgent, we remove the "Emotional Feedback Loop" that plagues retail participants. This section establishes the "Circuit Breakers"—the hard limits on daily drawdown—that prevent the system from "overgrazing" its capital base during periods of extreme volatility.

"So What?" Layer

The architecture prioritizes the "Risk-Reward Edge" over the "Probability Edge." Technically, a system designed for a 2R, 5R, or 10R magnitude of wins is superior for AI-driven survival because it allows for a low win-rate (30-45%) to still produce significant compounding wealth. By focusing on the magnitude of wins rather than the frequency of "perceived" success, we build a robust framework that thrives on market volatility rather than being destroyed by it. The "Warden" doesn't just manage risk; it ensures that the "Tuition of the Market" is never high enough to threaten the total net worth.

Connective Tissue

Transitioning from individual position risk and portfolio circuit breakers, the system now expands its scope to encompass a "Total Wealth" view, integrating non-correlated assets to maintain macro-equilibrium.
