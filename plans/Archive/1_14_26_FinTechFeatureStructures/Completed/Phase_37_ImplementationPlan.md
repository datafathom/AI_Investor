### 37.2 Neo4j Non-Correlation Edges `[x]`

**Acceptance Criteria**: Explicitly map `NON_CORRELATED` relationships in Neo4j. (e.g., `GOLD` -[:NON_CORRELATED]-> `SPY`). Optimize portfolio to maximize these edges.

```cypher
MATCH (p:PORTFOLIO)-[:OWNS]->(a:ASSET)
MATCH (a)-[r:CORRELATED_WITH]->(b:ASSET)
WHERE r.coefficient > 0.8
RETURN a, b, "Duplicate Risk"
```
