## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide, protect, and improve our Service.

---

## 2. Types of Cookies We Use

### 2.1 Essential Cookies
**Purpose**: Required for the Service to function  
**Examples**: Authentication, session management, security  
**Can be disabled**: No (Service will not function without these)

### 2.2 Analytics Cookies
**Purpose**: Understand how visitors use our Service  
**Examples**: Page views, time spent, user flows  
**Can be disabled**: Yes (through browser settings)

### 2.3 Functional Cookies
**Purpose**: Remember your preferences and settings  
**Examples**: Language, theme, layout preferences  
**Can be disabled**: Yes (but preferences will not be saved)

### 2.4 Advertising Cookies
**Purpose**: Deliver relevant advertisements  
**Examples**: Ad targeting, conversion tracking  
**Can be disabled**: Yes (opt-out available)

## 3. Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services that may set cookies:
- **Google Analytics**: Website analytics
- **Stripe**: Payment processing
- **Sentry**: Error tracking
- **Advertising Partners**: Targeted advertising

---

## 4. Managing Cookies

### 4.1 Browser Settings
You can manage cookies through your browser settings:
- Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
- Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies
- Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
- Edge: Settings → Privacy → Cookies

### 4.2 Opt-Out Tools
- Google Analytics: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
- Advertising: http://www.aboutads.info/choices/
- Network Advertising: http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/

---

## 5. Cookie Retention

- **Session Cookies**: Deleted when you close your browser
- **Persistent Cookies**: Remain until expiration or deletion
- **Maximum Retention**: 2 years (unless required by law)
