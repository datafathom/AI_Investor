## 1. General Trading Risks

### 1.1 Market Risk
- Securities prices fluctuate and may decline
- Market conditions are unpredictable
- Economic events can cause significant losses
- Past performance does not guarantee future results

### 1.2 Liquidity Risk
- Some securities may be difficult to sell
- Market conditions may limit liquidity
- You may not be able to exit positions when desired

### 1.3 Volatility Risk
- Prices can change rapidly
- High volatility increases risk of loss
- Sudden market movements can cause significant losses

---

## 2. Algorithmic Trading Risks

### 2.1 System Failures
- Technical failures may prevent order execution
- System errors may cause unintended trades
- Network issues may delay or prevent trades
- Software bugs may cause losses

### 2.2 Over-Optimization
- Strategies may perform well in backtesting but fail in live trading
- Market conditions change, making strategies ineffective
- Historical performance does not guarantee future results

### 2.3 Execution Risk
- Orders may not execute at expected prices
- Slippage may occur, especially in volatile markets
- Partial fills may affect strategy performance

---

## 3. Leverage and Margin Risks

### 3.1 Margin Trading
- Margin trading amplifies both gains and losses
- You may be required to deposit additional funds
- Margin calls may force liquidation of positions
- You may lose more than your initial investment

### 3.2 Leverage Risk
- Leverage increases risk of significant losses
- Small price movements can cause large losses
- Leveraged positions may be liquidated automatically

---

## 4. Specific Product Risks

### 4.1 Options Trading
- Options can expire worthless
- Time decay reduces option value
- Complex strategies carry additional risks
- Unlimited loss potential for certain strategies

### 4.2 Futures Trading
- Futures involve substantial leverage
- Margin requirements may change
- Contract expiration creates additional risk
- Physical delivery may be required

### 4.3 Cryptocurrency Trading
- Extreme volatility
- Regulatory uncertainty
- Security risks (hacking, theft)
- Limited regulatory protection

## 10. Recommendations

### 10.1 Education
- Educate yourself about trading
- Understand the risks involved
- Start with paper trading
- Use only risk capital

### 10.2 Diversification
- Diversify your investments
- Don't put all capital in one strategy
- Spread risk across different assets

### 10.3 Risk Management
- Set stop-loss orders
- Use position sizing
- Monitor your positions
- Have an exit strategy
