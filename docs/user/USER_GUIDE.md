## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. Visit the AI Investor website
2. Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner
3. Enter your email address and create a password
4. Verify your email address
5. Complete the onboarding flow

### Onboarding Flow

When you first sign up, you'll go through a quick onboarding process:

1. **Welcome**: Learn about AI Investor's features
2. **Experience Level**: Tell us about your trading experience
3. **Investment Goals**: Select your primary investment goals
4. **Risk Tolerance**: Choose your comfort level with risk
5. **Preferences**: Set your investment preferences
6. **Complete**: You're ready to start!

You can skip the onboarding at any time and complete it later in Settings.

## Dashboard Overview

### Main Dashboard

The main dashboard provides an overview of your portfolio and market activity:

- **Portfolio Value**: Current total value of your investments
- **Daily P&L**: Profit or loss for today

Use the top navigation menu to access:

1. Navigate to the Dashboard

## Trading

### Placing Orders

1. **Search for Symbol**: Use the search bar to find stocks
2. **Select Order Type**:
   - Market Order: Executes immediately at current price
   - Limit Order: Executes only at your specified price
   - Stop Order: Triggers when price reaches a threshold
3. **Enter Quantity**: Specify number of shares
4. **Review Order**: Check all details
5. **Confirm**: Submit your order

### Order Types Explained

- **Market Order**: Fastest execution, current market price
- **Limit Order**: Price protection, may not execute
- **Stop Loss**: Automatic sell if price drops
- **Trailing Stop**: Adjusts with price movement

### Viewing Order History

1. Go to Trading page
2. Click "Order History"
3. View all past orders
4. Filter by date, symbol, or status

## Analytics & Insights

### Performance Metrics

- **Total Return**: Overall portfolio performance
- **Sharpe Ratio**: Risk-adjusted returns
- **Beta**: Market correlation
- **Alpha**: Excess returns vs. benchmark

### Risk Analysis

- **Value at Risk (VaR)**: Potential losses
- **Maximum Drawdown**: Largest peak-to-trough decline
- **Volatility**: Price fluctuation measure
- **Correlation**: Asset relationships

### Reports

Generate reports:

1. Navigate to Analytics
2. Select report type
3. Choose date range
4. Click "Generate Report"
5. Download as PDF or CSV

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Can't Log In

1. Check your email and password
2. Click "Forgot Password" to reset
3. Check your email for reset link
4. Clear browser cache and cookies

#### Orders Not Executing

1. Check order type and price
2. Verify sufficient funds
3. Check market hours
4. Review order status

#### Portfolio Not Updating

1. Refresh the page
2. Check your internet connection
3. Clear browser cache
4. Contact support if issue persists
