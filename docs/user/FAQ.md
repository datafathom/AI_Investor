# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is AI Investor?

AI Investor is an intelligent investment platform that uses artificial intelligence to help you make better investment decisions. It provides portfolio management, trading capabilities, and advanced analytics.

### Is AI Investor free?

AI Investor offers both free and premium plans. The free plan includes basic features, while premium plans unlock advanced analytics, AI predictions, and priority support.

### Is my money safe?

Yes. AI Investor uses bank-level encryption and security measures. We never store your banking credentials, and all transactions are processed through secure, regulated brokers.

## Account & Security

### How do I reset my password?

1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset instructions
4. Follow the link to create a new password

### How do I enable two-factor authentication?

1. Go to Settings
2. Select "Security"
3. Click "Enable 2FA"
4. Follow the setup instructions
5. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app

### Can I change my email address?

Yes. Go to Settings > Account and update your email address. You'll need to verify the new email.

## Trading & Orders

### What order types are supported?

- Market Orders
- Limit Orders
- Stop Loss Orders
- Trailing Stop Orders
- Advanced order types (available in premium)

### When do orders execute?

- Market orders: Immediately during market hours
- Limit orders: When price reaches your limit
- Stop orders: When trigger price is reached

### What are market hours?

Regular trading hours are 9:30 AM - 4:00 PM ET, Monday through Friday. Extended hours trading is available for premium users.

### Can I cancel an order?

Yes, you can cancel pending orders before they execute. Go to Order History and click "Cancel" on the order.

## Technical Issues

### The app is slow or not loading

1. Check your internet connection
2. Clear browser cache and cookies
3. Try a different browser

5. Contact support if issue persists

1. Check browser console for errors
2. Disable ad blockers
3. Enable JavaScript
4. Try a different browser

1. Update to the latest app version
2. Restart the app
3. Clear app cache
